The Challenge

The National Maritime Intelligence-Integration Office (NMIO) in coordination with the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) Science & Technology Directorate (S&T), and the U.S. Coast Guard needed a way to automate and fuse complex and disparate real-time data sources to determine ship risk for their maritime safety mission.

The Solution

We used our open architecture design and machine learning to determine individual ship risk through the fusion of real-time terrestrial AIS data and real-time data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administrationâ€™s (NOAA) National Data Buoy Center (NDBC).

The Result

Our solution automated and fused real-time risk analytics for over 25,000 ships and over 1,300 buoys world-wide
